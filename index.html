The Best Pipe Manufacturing Company in India

Astral Pipes was established in 1996. It is one of the best pipe manufacturing companies in India. Its primary aim was to manufacture pro-India plumbing and drainage systems. Astral Pipes covered the needs of millions of houses and added extra mileage to India’s developing real estate fraternity with the hallmark of unbeaten quality. Astral Pipes is equipped with production facilities at Santej and Dholka in Gujarat and Hosur in Tamil Nadu. These factories manufacture plumbing systems, drainage systems, agriculture systems, fire sprinkler piping systems, industrial piping and electrical conduit pipes with all kinds of necessary fittings.

India’s plastic piping category is essentially an unorganised sector and a battle ground where many small players are still competing. The category is characterised by very low consumer involvement, a huge dependence on plumbers and above all, low awareness amongst end users.

Best CPVC Pipe Company

Astral Pipes specialises in manufacturing world class plumbing, drainage, fire sprinkler and conduit piping systems for both residential and commercial applications and CPVC piping system for industrial applications. Astral Pipes also has column and pressure piping systems for agriculture. We adopt latest international technologies and provide quality piping solutions for varied needs of the market.

Astral Pipes is a pioneer of CPVC Pipes in India with 17 years of expertise. We have a diversified range of products on offer. The category has now achieved a global benchmark. Our network has over 750 distributors and 28,000 dealers across India.
